About Inter

The Inter Video Game Arcade was developed originally in 2011 as a digital innovation to local arcades in Washington, Seattle. It was developed by a team of 11 people and was built in only a few days. The Inter Video Game Arcade develops and produces a variety of games such as 2D platformers, classic shooters, and more!
